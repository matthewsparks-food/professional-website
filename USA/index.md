# USA Recipes

Welcome to the USA section. Here you’ll find classic American dishes, popular recipes from around the country, and a list of ingredients for each dish so you can try making them at home.

- Mac and Cheese  
    - Ingredients: elbow macaroni, butter, flour, milk, cheddar cheese, salt, pepper
- Tacos
  - Ingredients: taco shells or tortillas, ground beef or chicken, lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, salsa
- Pizza  
    - Ingredients: pizza dough, tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, olive oil, pepperoni or vegetables
- Chicken and Waffles
  - Ingredients: fried chicken, waffle mix or waffles, butter, syrup, salt, pepper
- Chili  
    - Ingredients: ground beef, beans, tomatoes, chili powder, onions, garlic, salt, pepper
- Cornbread
  - Ingredients: cornmeal, flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, milk, eggs, butter or oil
- Fried Chicken  
    - Ingredients: chicken pieces, flour, eggs, buttermilk, salt, pepper, oil for frying
- BBQ Ribs
  - Ingredients: pork ribs, BBQ sauce, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder
- Hot Dogs  
    - Ingredients: hot dog sausages, hot dog buns, ketchup, mustard, relish
- Burgers
  - Ingredients: ground beef patties, burger buns, cheese, lettuce, tomato, pickles, ketchup, mustard
- Apple Pie  
    - Ingredients: pie crust, apples, sugar, cinnamon, butter, flour
